Technical Reference Card

1. Logarithmic Stabilizer (Target Transform)

y_trans = ln(y + 1)
y_orig = exp(y_trans) - 1

Stabilizes variance and limits the leverage of major monthly claim anomalies during training.

2. Pinball Loss Function (Quantile Estimation)

L_q(y, y_pred) = max[ q * (y - y_pred), (q - 1) * (y - y_pred) ]

Used to generate customized confidence intervals (e.g. q=0.1 and q=0.9) to provide Finance with bounded budgeting options.

3. Stationarity Benchmark (Augmented Dickey-Fuller)

Δy_t = α + β y_{t-1} + δ t + Σ( γ_i Δy_{t-i} ) + ε_t

Tests the null hypothesis of a time-series unit root. Rejection confirms statistical stationarity.

4. Weighted Absolute Percentage Error (WAPE)

WAPE = Σ | y_t - y_pred_t | / Σ y_t

Prevents division-by-zero or inflation issues common in standard MAPE when monthly OON volume temporarily drops.
