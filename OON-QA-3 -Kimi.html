Model comparison at a glance

  • Naive: 24.1% MAPE — sanity floor
  • Seasonal Naive: 21.8% — baseline
  • SARIMA: 18.5% — statistical baseline
  • Random Forest: 14.2% — candidate
  • XGBoost: 12.6% — final (chosen)

Key metrics & what they mean

  • MAPE: % error, Finance-friendly
  • RMSE: punishes big misses
  • WAPE: stable when costs are low
  • Directional accuracy: did we call "rising"?
  • R²: 0.88 — model explains 88% variance

Feature selection — 4 passes

  • Pass 1: Drop constants & leakage
  • Pass 2: Correlation filter |r| > 0.95
  • Pass 3: XGBoost gain (training fold)
  • Pass 4: Permutation importance (validation)

Alert logic (production)

  • 3-month forecast rises > 12%
  • AND lower confidence bound also rises
  • → 🔴 HIGH alert to Finance + Network
  • Fallback: Seasonal Naive if pipeline fails
